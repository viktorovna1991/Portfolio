Why it matters

Our vision is a world where animal welfare matters and all animal cruelty has ended.

There is a vital relationship between people and animals, because a sustainable future for the planet can only be achieved if both animals and people are part of the solution.

Together, we can move the world to protect animals.

Our mission is to create a better world for animals

  • We end the needless suffering of animals
  • We influence decision makers to put animals on the global agenda
  • We help the world see how important animals are to all of us
  • We inspire people to change the live of animals for the better
  • We move the world to protect animals
